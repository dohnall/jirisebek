Annexe 1.31: Transforming (4/6)

Knowledge-based products and services

The research nature and “formal outputs” of FLA (see Section 3.3) are key elements contributing to the transformation of current and future knowledge. Furthermore, some results of FLA have an impact on knowledge-based products (e.g. books, research papers, white papers, case studies, databases, reports, etc.) as well as knowledge-based services (e.g. research consultancy, risk management, software and technology development, procurement advice, etc.).
